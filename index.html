Hanson Harbor Properties

Looking for a large, recreational ranch in sunny Eastern Washington bordering on Lake Roosevelt with spectacular scenery and recreation? We have two large properties just for you! Hanson Harbor Ranch (2020 acres) and Whitestone Heights (670 acres) are truly special and enjoy spectacular lake and mountain views. Both properties are upriver from Grand Coulee Dam on the Columbia River in Lincoln County, Washington and accessed from Wilbur, Washington. Wilbur is about an hour and a half drive West of Spokane and four hours East of Seattle. Each affords access to water sports, recreational boating and fishing, along with hiking, biking, riding, hunting and birding opportunities. The two properties differ in size and are not adjacent to one another, but the features are very similar and will appeal to a variety of different purchasers or purchasing groups.


Hanson Harbor Ranch is offered by its sole owner, Hanson Harbor, Inc.. Whitestone Heights is a collaborative offering by certain members of the Hanson Family and the Jones Family.
